PAATH

Performance Analytics for Asphalt Technology & Highway

PAATH (Performance Analytics for Asphalt Technology & Highway) is an AI-powered software that leverages machine learning techniques to replace laboratory testing methods in transportation and highway engineering. Designed for QA/QC applications, PAATH provides accurate and reliable, enhancing efficiency in performance evaluation and quality assurance while reducing time and costs associated with conventional testing.
